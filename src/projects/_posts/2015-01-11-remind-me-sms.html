Remind Me SMS

The Idea

Remind Me SMS lets you send SMS messages worldwide on a scheduled basis. You can use this service whenever you need to be sure you'll send a message to someone on a specific date.

About

It's very easy to use this service. You just enter recipient's a cell phone number (your friend, colleague, or even your boss). Then you enter a short message, that will be sent. Also, you can add several recipients if you want them to receive the same message. And then you submit your request.

We don't collect cell phone numbers for any spamming activity and don't sell them for any 3rd party person.

Technology Stack

  • Parse.com: our back-end & hosting
  • Twilio: our SMS gateway
